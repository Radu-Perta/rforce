Our Story

Dedicated to Excellence Since 2005

rForce Auto Service was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide honest, reliable, and professional auto repair and maintenance services that customers can trust. What started as a small family operation has grown into a full-service automotive center that the community relies on.

Our founder, Perta Radu, began his career as a mechanic in 1990 and spent 15 years honing his craft before opening R Force Auto Service. His dedication to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction established the core values that still guide our business today.

Over the years, we've expanded our facilities and services to meet the evolving needs of our customers and their vehicles. Today, we're proud to offer comprehensive automotive care using state-of-the-art equipment and techniques, while maintaining the personalized service that has been our hallmark from the beginning.
