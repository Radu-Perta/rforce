Timing Chain Replacement

Ensure your engine's longevity with our professional timing chain replacement service. The timing chain is critical for synchronizing your engine's valves and pistons for optimal performance.

What's Included in Our Timing Chain Replacement Service?

Our comprehensive timing chain replacement service ensures your engine runs smoothly and reliably. Our certified technicians will:

  • Remove necessary components to access the timing chain
  • Replace old timing chain with a high-quality new one
  • Replace timing chain tensioners and guides
  • Verify correct engine timing after installation
  • Inspect related components for wear
  • Replace front crankshaft seal and oil pump seal if needed
  • Perform quality check to ensure proper installation
  • Test-drive vehicle to verify normal operation
